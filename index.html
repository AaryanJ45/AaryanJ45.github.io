Topic: The topic of our project is the Analysis of Battery/Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (BEV's and PHEV's) Registrations in Washington State.

Some questions we are trying to answer: Firstly, we want to do some trend analysis, looking at how the registrations of BEVs and PHEVs have changed over time in the state of Washington. We also want to look at what are the most popular models of BEVs and PHEVs in Washington using popularity and diversity statistics.
